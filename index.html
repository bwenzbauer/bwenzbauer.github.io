About Me


I've grown up in Syracuse, Utah and graduated High School in 2021, following after I went to Davis Technical College to focus on Diesel Mechanics, after completeing the program I went out and got my 'dream job' working on trains for Utah Transit Authority. After doing that for a little bit I relaized that this career path was not for me and decided quickly I needed to make a change. I moved onto a full time gig at a local mechanic shop for several months while working my part-time custodial job in addition to that to maximize my efforts while not in school. During that time I decided I should go back to school with focus on another one of my favorite interests, working with computers in every aspect. I took up a Web and Graphic Design class back at the technical college that I finished and transfered a good majority of what I had accomplished there to finish out my degree at Weber State University. I think my main take away here is that it's okay to try stuff out and making mistakes is truely okay as that's the best way to learn and figure out the right answer.
